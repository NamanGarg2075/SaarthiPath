Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to personalized career guidance that goes beyond generic advice.

The Problem

Many college students struggle to navigate their learning journey because of scattered resources, lack of personalized guidance, and limited real-world mentorship. They often waste time choosing what to study, lose motivation without accountability, and miss out on practical advice from experienced seniors and alumni. This gap leaves students underprepared for internships, jobs, and personal growth.

Our Vision

To empower every college student with a clear, personalized, and guided learning journey — supported by curated resources, daily mentorship, and a nurturing community — so they can unlock their true potential and step confidently into the world of work and life.

Long-term Goals

SaarthiPath aims to become the most trusted and accessible guidance ecosystem for college students, offering personalized learning paths, daily mentorship, and real-world opportunities like internships and projects. Over time, it envisions building a strong network of mentors and alumni, making quality guidance available to students from all backgrounds — especially those in tier 2 and tier 3 colleges. By integrating data-driven insights and focusing on holistic growth beyond academics, SaarthiPath aspires to reshape student journeys, bridge the gap between college and career, and eventually expand its impact to students globally.

At SaarthiPath, mentorship works on two levels:
Senior Mentors — Experienced seniors and industry experts who conduct group sessions, webinars, and doubt-clearing classes to share practical insights and guidance.

Peer Mentors — Friendly mentors who stay in daily touch with you, track your progress, help you follow your personalized learning plan, and keep you motivated when you feel stuck.

Together, this dual mentorship model ensures you get both expert advice and daily support — so you’re never lost or alone on your learning journey

Most platforms just give you courses or content — we go much beyond that. SaarthiPath offers:
Personalized, date-wise learning paths tailored to your goals, not one-size-fits-all videos.

Daily mentorship and progress tracking — so you stay consistent, motivated, and never feel lost.

Dual mentorship model: senior mentors for expert group sessions + peer mentors for everyday support.

Real accountability: if you miss tasks, someone notices, follows up, and helps you get back on track.

Focus on holistic growth: not just technical skills, but also communication, mindset, and real-world readiness.

In short, SaarthiPath isn’t just about “what to study,” it’s about walking every step of the journey with you — till you succeed.
